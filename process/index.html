How the process works

Clear steps. No guesswork. Each project follows a structured plan so expectations are set before installation begins.

1

On-site consultation + space mapping

The space is reviewed in person. Layout, materials, and device placement are evaluated. A LiDAR scan is used to map the space for accurate Wi-Fi and camera planning. That map is then used inside UniFi Design Center to model coverage before anything is installed.

2

Design review + equipment plan

A follow-up meeting walks through the Design Center layout and coverage plan. Equipment is reviewed, placement is confirmed, and a clear list of hardware is provided. No surprises before installation.

3

Installation

Cabling, racks, access points, cameras, gateways, and switches are installed cleanly and labeled properly. The goal is a finished system that looks organized and professional.

4

Configuration, training, and handoff

The system is configured, tested, and reviewed. Wi-Fi is verified, cameras are confirmed working, and access is demonstrated. The client receives login access and a walkthrough so the system is easy to manage.

5

Continued administration (optional)

Ongoing remote administration is available if desired. This can include monitoring, updates, and faster response if issues arise. Clients can choose to manage everything themselves or keep continued support in place.
