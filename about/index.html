What we do

Slade Networks designs, installs, and supports modern network infrastructure for homes and small businesses across the Finger Lakes. Most projects use UniFi for a single-pane dashboard across routing, switching, Wi-Fi, cameras, and VoIP — with documentation and configuration that stays maintainable.

Whether you’re building from scratch or fixing coverage, reliability, or visibility issues, the approach is the same: proper planning, clean cabling and mounting, correct RF design, and predictable configs with sensible security defaults.
